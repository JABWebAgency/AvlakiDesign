What is Avlaki?

With 20+ years of working in the landscaping/ excavation industry's, we have seen some issues. From simply french drains to elaborate exterior sump systems. Avlaki, uses old and new technology to create a system that will not only solve your problem today but you won't have to ever think about it again.
