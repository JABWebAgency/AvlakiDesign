What is Avlaki?

You may be wondering, what does “Avlaki” mean? It’s a Greek word that translates to “furrow” or “drain.” Because our company specializes in water management, we chose a name that directly reflects what we do best—drainage.

With over 20 years of experience in landscaping and excavation, we’ve encountered every type of drainage challenge, from basic French drains to complex exterior sump systems. At Avlaki, we blend trusted, time-tested techniques with modern technology to design solutions that not only address your immediate needs but also give you lasting peace of mind. When you choose Avlaki, you’re choosing a system built to stand the test of time—so you can stay focused on what matters most.
