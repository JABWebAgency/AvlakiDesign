Drain Services

Curtain and French Drains

Avlaki specializes in installing curtain and French drains to efficiently manage water flow and protect your property from water damage. These systems are designed to prevent flooding, safeguard foundations, and ensure long-term water management for a dry, secure environment.

  • Avlaki installs curtain drains around your property to redirect excess water away from critical areas like foundations and basements. This system helps prevent water buildup, reducing the risk of flooding and soil erosion.

  • Our French drain systems efficiently channel water away from your foundation or landscaped areas, preventing water damage and pooling. Designed with perforated pipes and gravel, French drains provide a long-lasting solution to groundwater management.
