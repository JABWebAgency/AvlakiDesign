Drain Services

Curtain and French Drains

Curtain Drains

At Avlaki, we specialize in installing curtain drains to help manage groundwater around your property. Curtain drains are typically placed along foundations or slopes to intercept and redirect water before it can cause damage. This system is highly effective in preventing water from pooling near sensitive areas, protecting your foundation and landscaping from potential flooding.

French Drains

We also offer the installation of French drains, a reliable solution for managing excess water around your property. French drains consist of a trench filled with gravel and a perforated pipe that channels water away from areas prone to flooding. This system helps reduce water buildup and prevents damage to your foundation, keeping your property safe and dry throughout the year.
