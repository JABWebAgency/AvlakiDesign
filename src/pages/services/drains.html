Drain Services

Curtain and French Drains

Avlaki specializes in installing curtain and French drains to efficiently manage water flow and protect your property from water damage. These systems are designed to prevent flooding, safeguard foundations, and ensure long-term water management for a dry, secure environment.

  • Our French drain systems efficiently channel water away from from swales, low points and really any place water is not wanted. French drains can be used in many different ways around your property. Designed with needle punched filter fabric, perforated drain tile, and no fines round aggregates, our french drain systems are built to last and perform at the highest level.

  • Avlaki installs curtain drains to capture subsurface movement of water. This typically takes place on hillsides where extreme separation of soils is present. Water moving subsurface requires a precise attention to detail, in order to capture and redirect. Curtain drains are very similar to french drains in construction, however they are larger in design.
