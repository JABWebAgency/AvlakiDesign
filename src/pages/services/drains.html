Drain Services

Avlaki specializes in installing and maintaining curtain drains and French drains to effectively manage groundwater and surface water. Curtain drains are installed around the perimeter of a property to redirect water away from critical areas, preventing flooding and soil erosion. These drains are particularly useful in areas with high water tables or poor drainage conditions.

French drains, on the other hand, are designed to handle excess water in more concentrated areas by channeling it through a perforated pipe buried in gravel. This solution is ideal for redirecting water around foundations, basements, or lawns, ensuring a dry and safe environment.
