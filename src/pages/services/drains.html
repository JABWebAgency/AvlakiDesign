Service

Customized Water Management Solutions for Every Property

At Avlaki, we specialize in addressing your unique water flow needs, from curtain drains to French drains, ensuring optimal performance and long-lasting results. No matter the challenge, we provide solutions designed for your property’s specific requirements.

Our Process

  • Consultation & Assessment: We start with a thorough inspection of your property to understand the drainage issues, whether it’s water pooling, poor drainage flow, or soil erosion, and discuss the best solution, such as a curtain or French drain.
  • Custom Drainage Plan: Based on our assessment, we develop a tailored plan. For areas prone to water accumulation, we may recommend French drains, while curtain drains are ideal for preventing water runoff from infiltrating sensitive areas like foundations.
  • Installation of French & Curtain Drains: Our expert team carries out the installation of French drains or curtain drains with precision. We ensure that the systems are placed correctly for maximum effectiveness, using high-quality materials to guarantee longevity.
  • Final Inspection & Testing: After installation, we conduct a comprehensive inspection and testing to ensure proper drainage functionality. We make sure your French or curtain drains are operating as intended, protecting your property from water damage.
