Well Services

Avlaki provides expert installation and maintenance of exterior sump wells and dry wells to address water accumulation and protect your property from flooding. Exterior sump wells are designed to collect excess water from around the foundation and direct it into a sump pump, which then channels the water away from your home. This system is ideal for properties prone to waterlogging, ensuring that basements and foundations remain dry.

Dry wells, on the other hand, are underground structures that allow water to percolate into the ground gradually, making them an effective solution for areas with poor drainage. By installing these systems, Avlaki helps prevent water pooling, reduces soil erosion, and preserves the integrity of your property’s structure and landscaping. Whether it’s managing stormwater or groundwater, Avlaki’s well solutions provide efficient, reliable protection for your home or business.
