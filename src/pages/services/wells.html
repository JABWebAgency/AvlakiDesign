Water Collection

Not all drainage can be flow naturally from a problem area. At times drainage needs help from a pump or percolation. We have you covered.

  • Avlaki installs exterior sump basins where water can not be directed with the assistance of gravity. These systems direct water to a large basin and with the assistance of a pump the water is then redirected to a new location. These systems are designed to mitigate the toughest of water problems.

  • Our dry wells are designed with a capture basin that is allowed to percolate to the soils. This effective solution works very well when sandy soils are present. A dry well is a great option when water needs to be maintained within the boundaries of the property.
