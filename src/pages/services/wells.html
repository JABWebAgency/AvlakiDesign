Well Services

Exterior Sump Wells and Dry Wells

Exterior Sump Wells

At Avlaki, we offer the installation of exterior sump wells to help manage excess water around your property. Exterior sump wells are designed to collect and store groundwater before it can cause flooding or damage to your foundation. Equipped with a pump, the system efficiently moves water away from the home, providing long-term protection against water buildup and ensuring a dry, secure environment for your property.

Dry Wells

We also specialize in dry wells, a sustainable solution for draining excess water on your property. A dry well is an underground system that collects and temporarily stores water, allowing it to slowly percolate into the surrounding soil. This solution is ideal for areas with poor drainage or where water needs to be diverted away from structures, helping to prevent pooling and erosion while maintaining a healthy landscape.
