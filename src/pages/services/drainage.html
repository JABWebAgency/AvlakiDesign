Downspouts and Foundation Drainage

Avlaki specializes in foundation and downspout drainage solutions to protect your property from water damage. By directing surface and subsurface water away from your foundation, we prevent movement and premature sump failure, ensuring the long-term safety and stability of your home or business.

  • Our foundation drainage solutions are designed to eliminate water next to your foundation that can cause unwanted movement of patios, deck footings and landscape. Also creates excessive cycle time on sump pump. By effectively managing water flow around your foundation, we ensure a dry, stable environment and safeguard the integrity of your property.

  • Avlaki designs and installs custom downspout systems to efficiently direct rainwater away from your foundation. This prevents water accumulation around your property, reducing the risk of flooding and long-term structural damage
