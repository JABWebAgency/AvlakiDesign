Grading/Excavating Services

Grading and Excavating

Avlaki’s grading and excavating services ensure your property is properly sloped for optimal water flow and drainage. By preparing your site with precision, we prevent water accumulation and protect your foundation from potential damage.

  • Avlaki’s grading services ensure that your property has the correct slope to direct water away from your foundation. Proper grading is key to preventing water pooling, protecting your landscape, and ensuring long-term structural integrity.

  • Our excavating services provide the precise site preparation needed for drainage systems and other water management solutions. With advanced equipment and expertise, we ensure your property is ready for efficient water flow and drainage infrastructure installation.
