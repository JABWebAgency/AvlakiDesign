Drain Services

Grading and Excavating

Grading

At Avlaki, our grading services are designed to improve the flow of water around your property. By reshaping the land to ensure proper slope and drainage, we help prevent water from pooling near foundations, walkways, or other critical areas. Our expert team works with precision to create a grade that directs water away from your home, reducing the risk of flooding, erosion, and water damage.

Excavating

We also offer professional excavating services to support various drainage solutions and property improvements. Whether it's digging trenches for drainage systems, removing soil, or preparing land for new construction, our experienced crew uses advanced equipment to get the job done efficiently and safely. With our excavating services, we ensure your property is properly prepared for effective water management and long-lasting stability.
