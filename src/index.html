About Decks By Kip

At Decks by Kip, we bring three decades of unparalleled expertise to the world of deck construction. Established in 1990, our family-owned business has been transforming outdoor spaces with exceptional craftsmanship and unwavering dedication. We take pride in our rich history of creating beautiful, functional decks that stand the test of time.

As industry pioneers, we've evolved with the latest trends and technologies, ensuring that each deck we build is a masterpiece of design and durability. Our commitment to excellence extends from the initial concept to the final hammering of the last nail, guaranteeing a seamless and stress-free experience for our clients.

With 30 years of expertise, Decks by Kip turns your dream deck into reality. Join satisfied clients and experience our passion for outdoor excellence.

Kip Mueller CEO-Founder
